Over 100,000 people use this browser extension — are you one of them?

If *Level-Up for Dynamics 365 / Power Apps* isn't pinned to your browser yet, you're working on hard mode.

<!--more-->

It works in Chrome, Edge, and Firefox and acts as a one-click toolbox for every model-driven app. Here's why it's earned a permanent spot in my workflow.

## Why You Need It

**Logical Names on demand** — see schema names for every field, tab, and section right on the form, without digging through the customisation editor. Essential for scripting or Power Fx.

**God Mode** — reveal hidden controls, make required fields optional, and edit read-only fields while you prototype or troubleshoot. This alone is worth the install.

**One-click Record ID and URLs** — copy deep links and GUIDs without hunting through the address bar. Massively speeds up bug reports and documentation.

**Admin shortcuts** — jump straight to Advanced Find, Solutions, Security Roles, Performance Center, and more. No more clicking through menus.

**Quick diagnostics** — view record details and form performance to spot issues before your users do.

## Getting Started

1. Search **"Level-Up for Dynamics 365"** in your browser's extension store and install it.
2. Refresh any model-driven app and click the Level-Up icon.
3. Try Logical Names and God Mode first. You'll wonder how you ever worked without them.

It's free, it's fast, and it genuinely changes how you work in Dynamics 365 day to day.
